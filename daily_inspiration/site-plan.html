Daily Inspiration Site Plan

Nathan Brower

WDD 130-3

Overview

Purpose

My website will have several inspirational quotes from the prophets and apostles of our day. Anytime you are needing a boost or a quick spiritual thought, you’ll be able to visit the website and find all the inspiration that you need. There will even be links to find the talks where the quote came from so that you can better your day as a result of visiting the website.

Audience

Members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and others who are looking for inspiration in their day.

Home page

Welcome to daily inspiration! We're glad you’re here. You'll find some of your favorite quotes from prophets and apostles here. Just click the links and enjoy.

Quote from President Nelson

spiritual awareness President Nelson April 2018 https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2018/04/revelation-for-the-church-revelation-for-our-lives?lang=eng “In coming days, it will not be possible to survive spiritually without the guiding, directing, comforting, and constant influence of the Holy Ghost. My beloved brothers and sisters, I plead with you to increase your spiritual capacity to receive revelation.”

Quote from President Nelson

President Nelson April 2022 https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2022/04/47nelson?lang=eng “We have never needed positive spiritual momentum more than we do now, to counteract the speed with which evil and the darker signs of the times are intensifying. Positive spiritual momentum will keep us moving forward amid the fear and uncertainty created by pandemics, tsunamis, volcanic eruptions, and armed hostilities. Spiritual momentum can help us withstand the relentless, wicked attacks of the adversary and thwart his efforts to erode our personal spiritual foundation.”

Quote from Elder Villar

Elder Juan Pablo Villar April 2019 https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2019/04/52villar?lang=eng “Let’s accept the invitation of President Russell M. Nelson and intently come unto the Savior by identifying those muscles that need more spiritual activity and starting to exercise them. This is a long-distance race, a marathon, rather than a sprint, so do not forget those small but constant spiritual activities that will strengthen those important spiritual muscles. If we want to increase our faith, then let’s do things that require faith.”
